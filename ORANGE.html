about the project.


ORANGE Magazine is devoted to serving members of marginalized communities by fashioning an inclusive platform that allows for open discussions. As a student-run publication, it seeks to provide an inclusive outlet for content creators (writers, designers, photographers) to improve their skills.







my role.


I serve as a designer on ORANGE Magazine and create illustrations to better connect the reader with the content. Whether it be by striking visual interest or inciting a laugh, I strive to engage the reader and facilitate a higher level of enlightenment.







the challenge.


A large pillar of being part of the ORANGE family is practicing empathy. Because I have not endured all of the personal struggles ORANGE covers, I strive to understand the plight of others in my work. Whether this be by internalizing the written articles or conducting further research, I find my best work comes through stronger insights.
